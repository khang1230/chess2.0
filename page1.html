Chess

How to Set Up

Start with the Board,

Make sure the top-left and bottom-right corners are white squares

Add the Ponds

Place the Ronds on the second row

Add the Rooks

The Rooks will go in the corners

Add the Knights(Horse)

The Knights will go right next to the Rooks

Add the Bishops

The Bishops will go right next to the Knights

Add the Queen

The Queen will go on the square matching its color behind the ponds

Add the King

The King will go on the square that is opposite from its color (next to the queen)



Tip!!!

The eight vertical columns of the chess board are called files

&

The eight horizontal rows of the chess board are called ranks
